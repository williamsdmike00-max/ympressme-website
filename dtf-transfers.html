About DTF

What Are DTF Transfers?

Direct-to-Film (DTF) transfers are printed designs on a special film that adhere to virtually any fabric using a standard heat press. They produce vivid, full-color prints with a soft feel — no weeding, no screen setup, no minimum colors.

  • ✓ Works on any fabric — cotton, polyester, nylon, leather, canvas
  • ✓ Light & dark fabrics — no separate process needed
  • ✓ Full-color prints — unlimited colors, photorealistic gradients
  • ✓ No weeding — just peel and press in under 20 seconds
  • ✓ Wash-resistant — prints that last wash after wash

How to Apply Your Transfer

1
Pre-press your garment for 3–5 seconds to remove moisture and wrinkles.
2
Position the transfer film-side up on the garment.
3
Press at 300–320°F for 15–20 seconds with firm pressure.
4
Peel the film warm or cold — your design is done!
