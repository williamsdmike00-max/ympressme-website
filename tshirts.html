Artwork Specs

Design File Requirements

Follow these guidelines for the best possible print quality.

🖼️

File Formats

Preferred: PNG with transparent background
Also accepted: PDF, SVG
Avoid JPEG for designs with text or transparency.

🔍

Resolution

Minimum: 300 DPI at print size
Recommended: 300–600 DPI
Low-res files will produce blurry results.

📐

Dimensions

Full front: Up to 12" × 16"
Pocket: Up to 4" × 4"
Provide art at the exact final print size.

⚠️ Transparent Background Required: All designs should have a transparent background (not white). White backgrounds will print as white on your garment.
